The Problem

Traditional cricket academies face multiple challenges

  • Manual registration and player tracking processes
  • Inefficient payment management and record keeping
  • Poor communication between coaches and players
  • Lack of centralized data for performance analytics
  • Time-consuming administrative tasks
  • Data loss and duplication issues

Our Solution

A comprehensive role-based platform

🌐
